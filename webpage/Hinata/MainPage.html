Use Cases

Task Mode

In task mode, people can manage their coming task ordered by the due data. These tasks will attach to the calendar of users' cell phones and remind our users based on the remind time. Besides, our users could adjust the priority if the want.

Time Mode

In time mode, people can set up a peorid of time to do their works without touching their cellular phones. In addition, user can select maximun 10 minutes resume time in case of reciving phone calls or checking important emails. If users successfully finish a task, they will gain a piece of puzzle as rewards. However, if users touch their cell phones incorrectly, they will lose the task.

FAQ

What is the purpose of this app?

Ants is a mobile app that helps users to focus on their study and stay away from mobile phone addictions.

How can I use this app?

New users can create an account to use this app. Ants also provide the flexibility of using Facebook account to log in.

How does ants help people to stay concentrated?

Ants uses puzzle collections to help people keep cooncentrated. If people can stay away from mobile phones for a pre determined time, they can get a piece of puzzle. Ants uses puzzles as a kind of motivation to help users stay away from mobile phone addictions.

Does this app have some special features compared with existing apps?

Compared with other existing apps, Ants allows users to compete with each other, and this further motivates users to keep concentrated. The longer time a user can keep concentrated, the more pieces of puzzles the user can get as well as a higher ranking in the competition.
