Use Cases

Task Mode

In task mode, people can manage their coming task ordered by the due data. These tasks will attach to the calendar of users' cell phones and remind our users based on the remind time. Besides, our users could adjust the priority if the want.

Time Mode

In time mode, people can set up a peorid of time to do their works without touching their cellular phones. In addition, user can select maximun 10 minutes resume time in case of reciving phone calls or checking important emails. If users successfully finish a task, they will gain a piece of puzzle as rewards. However, if users touch their cell phones incorrectly, they will lose the task.
